About CodeVerse 2025

CodeVerse 2025 is an educational initiative organized under the guidance of SVYASA University, dedicated to empowering students from Sections A, B, and C with real-world coding skills. Through a series of expertly curated sessions, hands-on workshops, and full-stack projects, students learn essential technologies like Python, GitHub, Django, Bootstrap, and more.

Mission

To nurture future developers through structured sessions, project-based learning, and peer collaboration.

Vision

To build a student-driven community of full-stack developers who learn by doing and grow by sharing knowledge.

Technology

Python, Django, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, GitHub, SQLite, and deployment tools like Heroku or Netlify.

Powered by SVYASA University

Inspired by innovation. Backed by mentorship.
