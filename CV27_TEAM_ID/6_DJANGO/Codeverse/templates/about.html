About CodeVerse: Our Vision, Our People

Our Guiding Vision

"CodeVerse is more than just a program; it's a transformative journey crafted to foster holistic growth. We are dedicated to providing a hands-on, project-driven learning experience that seamlessly blends technological prowess with creative problem-solving and profound personal development. Our ultimate aim is to empower participants with the skills and mindset required for future-ready roles in a dynamic world."

Organized by the esteemed Department of Engineering and Technology at S-VYASA (Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana), CodeVerse is an intensive 3-week program. From July 14th to August 1st, 2025, participants will immerse themselves in cutting-edge technologies, guided by a curriculum that prioritizes practical application and real-world project development, running daily from 9:00 am to 4:30 pm.
