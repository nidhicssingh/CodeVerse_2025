About S‑VYASA University

Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana – A Global Yoga University

S‑VYASA Deemed to be University, located in Bengaluru, is India's first Yoga university. It is recognized by the UGC and has been accredited with an 'A+' grade by NAAC. The university blends ancient yogic science with modern research and higher education.

Founded in 2002, S‑VYASA has pioneered scientific validation of yoga through over 500+ research publications. It offers programs in Yoga, Health Sciences, Computer Science, Management, and Life Sciences, focusing on holistic well-being and innovation.

Global Recognition

Recognized globally for its research in Yoga and health sciences with partnerships in over 30 countries.

Research Excellence

Home to Anvesana – a research center with over 200 completed studies integrating yoga and medicine.

Campus & Academics

A 100-acre serene campus offering UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs with a unique blend of modern and Vedic knowledge.
