Program Overview

CodeVerse is an intensive three-week summer program hosted by the Department of Engineering and Technology at S-VYASA. It is meticulously designed for ambitious students ready to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry demands.

Our curriculum focuses on practical application, collaborative projects, and direct interaction with industry experts, ensuring that every participant graduates with a portfolio of skills and the confidence to tackle future-ready roles.

Our 3-Week Journey

A structured curriculum designed for maximum impact.

Week 1: Foundations

Building a strong base with Python, AWS cloud hosting, Git version control, and essential career-readiness skills.

Week 2: Development

Diving deep into Frontend and Full-Stack Development using React, gaining industry insights, and starting capstone projects.

Week 3: Innovation

Exploring Generative AI and showcasing your skills in a final Project Expo and an exciting 24-Hour Hackathon.
