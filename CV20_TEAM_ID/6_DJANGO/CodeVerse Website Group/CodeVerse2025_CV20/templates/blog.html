From the CodeVerse Chronicle

Industry Insights: Key Takeaways from Our Guest Speakers

July 22, 2025 by the CodeVerse Team

This week, we had the privilege of hosting several industry leaders who shared their invaluable knowledge with our participants. Vinay Kumar from Pearson India kicked things off with a powerful session on bridging the gap between campus and corporate life, emphasizing the need for practical skills and a proactive mindset.

Later in the week, Madamohan Rao of YourStory captivated the audience with his talk on "Igniting the Entrepreneurial Mindset," encouraging students to think like innovators and problem-solvers. These sessions have provided our participants with a clear roadmap for their future careers.

Hackathon Prep: Gearing Up for 24 Hours of Innovation

July 29, 2025 by the CodeVerse Team

The excitement is building as we approach the final week and our most anticipated event: the 24-Hour Hackathon. In partnership with IBM, we hosted a special preparation session to equip our teams with the tools and strategies they need to succeed.

Participants learned about effective brainstorming, rapid prototyping, and delivering a compelling pitch. The goal is not just to build an application, but to create a viable solution to a socially relevant problem. We can't wait to see what our talented teams will create!
